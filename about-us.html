Why do we exist?

'Brands' are carefully woven by meaningful stories

  • Story comes first to become the cornerstone of an effective marketing strategy.
  • It’s time to bid sayonara to noisy click-baits and flashy websites without a story. Content is marketing.

Future is remote

  • Distributed teams allow remote access to best talent available globally, and not just locally, democratising the way to superior talent inclusion
  • The content ecosystem needs an inviting, engaging and empowering workplace which is a cornerstone of every content creators’ profession.

Data brings transparency necessary for scale

  • In the hitherto unorganised content market, sans data collection, information died soon after transactions are complete. This leads to systemic information asymmetry leading to distortions in price, quality and deliverables in the market where trust is largely absent between all actors (Businesses, aggregators and content creators)
  • WhitePanda’s data driven proprietary tech brings delivery standardisation to our product offerings leading to reliable service at scale. We let the market forces establish new normals of fair pricing and quality standards.
